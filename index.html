Rules and Regulations

Booking and Payment

Appointment Scheduling: All appointments must be scheduled at least 24 hours in advance through our online booking system.

Payment Terms: Full payment of ₹950 is required at the time of booking to confirm your appointment.

Rescheduling and Cancellations:

Rescheduling is allowed up to 12 hours before the scheduled time without any additional charges.

Cancellations made at least 12 hours in advance will receive a full refund.

Cancellations made within 12 hours of the appointment will not be refunded, but the client can reschedule the session once without additional charges.

Session Conduct

Punctuality: Clients are expected to join the session on time. Delays on the client’s part will not extend the session beyond the scheduled 60 minutes.

Preparation: Clients should come prepared with specific questions or topics they wish to discuss to make the most of the session.

Professional Environment: Sessions will be conducted professionally. Any form of inappropriate behavior will result in immediate termination of the session without a refund.

Confidentiality

Privacy: All information shared during the consultation will be kept confidential and used solely to provide the service.

Recording: Sessions are not recorded by default.

Post-Session Support

Follow-Up Questions: Clients may send follow-up questions via email within 15 Days after the consultation. Responses will be provided within 72 hours. (on the same theme or Sessions)

Additional Sessions: Any additional consultation sessions must be booked separately following the same procedure and payment terms.

Sample Consultation Flow

Introduction (5 minutes): Brief introduction and outline of the session.

Main Consultation (50 minutes): Detailed discussion on the client's specific questions or topics.

Conclusion (5 minutes): Summary of the key points discussed and recommendations.
